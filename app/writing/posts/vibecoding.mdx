Hi, this writing talks about my experience of vibe coding for months.

`Vibe coding` is my personal way of being lazy while the learning curve goes down. No more Stack Overflow, less searching. You can solve bugs and add new features with just a few prompts.

The result? You won't even figure out where the problem is, let alone the solution. Worst case, you just hand off all issues to AI.

> In fact, it's messy if you don't even try to `read` and `understand` any lines of code. That's a path you'll never come back from.

## The Flip Side

In the dark, there's always a tiny light.

I think above all, this is an `education in failure`. But I sit in front of the monitor coding for hours and hours.

It's called `focusing`, and that's what you get. It's normal to get confused and distracted. Many things will attract you and make you lose focus.

## Learning With AI

If you learn with AI by asking `what` is this, `how` it works, `try` this way instead, `explain` this function to me, you'll feel how fast the project moves. It makes you read more about what AI coding is and learn with it together.

## My Advice

- Good results come with good productivity
- Use AI as a tool, not slave labor
- Note everything you learn
